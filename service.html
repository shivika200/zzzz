THEME CAKES

When the occasion is all about themes, why not the cakes? Filled with the goodness of frosting and flavours, our theme cakes are ready to enhance your theme of the day. Customize the cake of your preference and surprise your loved one with a wholesome moment.

TRUFFLE CAKE

The truffle is made with pure dark chocolate, fresh dairy cream and a dash of butter. The rich, moist and fudgy dense chocolate cake is also made with pure dark chocolate (instead of cocoa powder), butter and sugar. Tastes best on the day of purchase. Keep refrigerated.

PASTRIES

Pastry is baked food made with a dough of flour, water, and shortening (solid fats, including butter or lard) that may be savoury or sweetened. Sweetened pastries are often described as bakers' confectionery. The word "pastries" suggests many kinds of baked products made from ingredients such as flour, sugar, milk, butter, shortening, baking powder, and eggs. Small tarts and other sweet baked products are called pastries
